Self-Assessment Service

Welcome to the osteoarthritis self-assessment service. These series of questions will enable you have an informed discussion with your doctor and can serve as a benchmark in monitoring the progress of your condition.

You should select the joint affected and fill the form with your answers. Please print and keep your results, this information may be useful in your doctor appointment.

NOTE: Only members of the “OA Management Club”, will have their records analyzed and reported to them. Non-members can still use the Self-assessment service but their information will not be analyzed or retained.

Reminder system to book the next appointment
