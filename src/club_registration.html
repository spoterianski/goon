OA Management Club Registration

Welcome to the OA Management Club (Osteoarthritis Management Club) registration page, a free service provided to GO-ON® patients

Your doctor can enroll you in the OA Management Club with GO-ON®

The OA Management Club guides and supports you to be more active, and manages to control osteoarthritis of the knee, hip and/or shoulder.


Find below the Steps to joining the OA Management Club

1. Include your information in the registration form. Print and take the form to your next appointment.

2. Your doctor will complete filing in the form and will submit the form by fax to our offices.

3. Once enrolled, you will receive your OA Management Club welcome information

4. You will be invited to an initial self-evaluation of your osteoarthritis. We will tabulate the results to compare with future evaluations, and assess how well your osteoarthritis had been managed. A copy will be sent to you and your doctor

5. You will also benefit from our Reminder System for your next appointment.

6. Periodically you will receive educational materials as well as information and articles about osteoarthritis and tips on how to manage your osteoarthritis to live better by email.

7. Five (5) months after your initial registration and injection, we will invite you to do a self-assessment again to check your progress. We will share these results with you and your doctor. Your doctor can check if your condition is improving and can provide you with more adequate recommendations to improve the management of your osteoarthritis.


Print the registration form and bring it to your next appointment to be completed by your doctor and sent by fax.

Ask your doctor about the OA Management Club.
